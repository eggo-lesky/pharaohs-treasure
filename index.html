Treasure & Curse

How to play

Goal: Force your opponent to run out of cards.

On your turn: Tap your deck (blue) to flip the top card onto the Treasure Pile. The AI (orange) goes automatically.

The Curse: When a face card is played, the opponent gets chances to flip a face card back — 1 for a Jack, 2 for a Queen, 3 for a King. If they succeed, they claim the pile and the curse chains. If they fail, the face-card player claims the pile.

Combo: If two non-face cards of the same rank are played in a row, the Treasure Pile glows green — tap it fast to claim it! The AI may also notice and grab it.

Press "New Game" to start.
